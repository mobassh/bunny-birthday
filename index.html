“A surprise made just for you, Farah (Bunny) 🐰”

Tere jaise log baar baar nahi milte,
Na khoon ka rishta, na koi vaasta tha...
Phir bhi dil tujhe behen kehne ko chaha.

Har muskurahat mein tu saath thi,
Har khamoshi mein tu awaz thi...

Farah, tu meri duaon ka jazbaat thi. 🖤

Why You Matter

You’ve always been soft with others,
even when life wasn’t soft with you.
Your kindness, your curiosity, and the way you care —
it’s rare, Farah. That’s why you’re special.
That’s why I’m lucky to know you.
Not everyone deserves to be called ‘sister’,
but you earned that word — by being it.

You're Like...

🐈 Like a cat who trusts you — rare, warm, and real.
☕ A cup of karak chai with extra zafran — soft but unforgettable.
